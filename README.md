## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
**Price:** R0/month

#### Features:
- Basic business profile
- 3 gallery images
- 5 products in shop
- 1 image per product
- 3 product/services share links
- Limited sharing days (Mon-Fri only)
- Per listing: 2 products OR 3 images

#### Limitations:
- No scheduling for listings
- No advanced analytics
- Limited to weekday sharing only
- Cannot cancel anytime (subscription commitment)
- Setup fees may apply

---

### Premium Tier
**Price:** R149/month (or R1,192/year - 20% discount)

#### Features:
- Everything in Free
- 8 gallery images
- 20 products in shop
- 8 images per product
- 8 product/services share links
- Multi-platform link sharing (WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- Advanced analytics
- E-commerce features
- Per listing: 3 products OR 8 images
- Listing scheduling capability
- All-day sharing (no day restrictions)

#### Benefits:
- Expanded reach with more products
- Better analytics for business insights
- Schedule campaigns for optimal engagement
- Professional marketing tools

---

### Business Tier
**Price:** R299/month (or R2,392/year - 20% discount)

#### Features:
- Everything in Premium
- 12 gallery images
- Unlimited products in shop
- 12 images per product
- 20 product/services share links
- Advanced marketing channels
- Bulk campaign management
- Priority support (24/7 customer support access)
- Per listing: 4 products OR 12 images
- Listing scheduling capability
- All-day sharing (no day restrictions)

#### Benefits:
- Unlimited product catalog

- Priority customer support
